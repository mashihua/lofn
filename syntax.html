Identifiers

First of all, let's look at identifiers. Like Javascript, Identifiers is formed by Letters, Numbers, _ or $, and, numbers can't appear at the beginning of any identifier.

identifier = ( Letter | "_" | "$" ) ( Letter | Digit | "_" | "$" )*

The in identifier directly after operator is will be regarded as an operator. So, is in will be processed specially.
